# 2026-03-01 – Accounts Layer Design


## Objective
Design the accounts layer for the GrihaLedger OLTP system.

---

## Key Discussion

Evaluated ownership model for financial accounts.

Options considered:
- Accounts owned by household
- Accounts owned by users
- Hybrid model

Decision:
Accounts will be owned by users. Household views will aggregate
member accounts through the household_members relationship.

---

## Grain Definition

Table: raw.accounts

1 row = 1 financial account owned by exactly one user.

---

## Design Notes

Account types defined for Phase 1:
- savings
- credit
- cash
- loan
- investment

More granular classification will be added later if required.
